About Me

Who I Am

I'm Salma Adel, a UI/UX designer with a passion for crafting digital experiences that are as intuitive as they are visually captivating. I believe great design starts with empathy — understanding user needs, behaviors, and pain points to create solutions that truly resonate. From user research to high-fidelity prototyping, I bring ideas to life with a focus on clarity, consistency, and accessibility. My work is driven by a curiosity to simplify the complex and make technology feel more human. Whether it's a mobile app or a web platform, I design with purpose, ensuring every interaction leaves a lasting positive impression.
